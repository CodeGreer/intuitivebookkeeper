Packages

Every business entity is as unique as every business owner. So, while I offer standard bookkeeping packages, I also offer add-ons that can be bundled to create the exact customized package that serves your needs.


Essentials Package
Starting at $142/month

Perfect for a start-up or an established business that doesn’t have a lot of bookkeeping or reporting requirements, but needs help keeping things in order.

  • Quarterly bookkeeping, including classification of transactions and account reconciliations
  • 1 account (checking or credit) up to 50 transactions per quarter
  • Online accounting software subscription
  • Quarterly and annual financial reports
  • Quarterly 1-hour strategy session
  • Unlimited email support
Classic Package
Starting at $372/month

If you can’t keep up with your receipts and reconciliations, if you need to focus on growing your business instead of bookkeeping, and if you need monthly reports so you track your financial goals, then this package is for you.

  • Monthly bookkeeping, including classification of transactions and account reconciliations
  • Up to 2 accounts (checking/credit) up to 75 transactions per month
  • Online accounting software subscription
  • Receipt management subscription
  • Monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports
  • Monthly 1-hour strategy session
  • Unlimited email and phone support
Executive Package
Starting $582/month
  • Monthly bookkeeping, including classification of transactions and account reconciliations
  • Up to 3 accounts (checking/credit) up to 100 transactions per month
  • Online accounting software subscription
  • Receipt management subscription
  • Budgeting and cashflow management
  • Tax planning
  • Monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports
  • Monthly 1-hour strategy session
  • Unlimited email and phone support
