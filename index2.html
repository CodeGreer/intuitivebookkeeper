My Philosophy

I am here to eliminate your bookkeeping stress, plain and simple. Crushing your goals and obtaining your dreams cannot happen without a plan. The fact is, a universal pain point for entrepreneurs is…bookkeeping. Don’t be like most small businesses and fail because of poor cash management.

I use all of my strengths and skills to free up hours of your time doing things you don’t enjoy so that you can focus on your business and build meaningful relationships. Together we can formulate a plan to surpass your goals and make your dreams come to fruition. After all, isn’t that why we are in business for ourselves?

Just the Basics

  • Online and Easy: QuickBooks Online or Xero accounting software, accessible anytime, anywhere.
  • Know Your Numbers: Financial reports in easy, understandable language give you the information you need to plan and take action.
  • Document Management: Submit receipts by snapping a picture with your phone, and I’ll do the rest.
  • Encryption: All your data is safe by using bank-level encryption platforms.
  • No Contract: Month-to-month services, cancellable by either party with 30-days’ notice.
  • Fixed Monthly Fees: No surprises; to help control your costs.
  • Unlimited Communication: I’ll respond within 24 hours to your email. I’m here to serve you.
  • Strategy Sessions: At agreed-on intervals, I’ll provide financial data to assess your business’ performance.
  • Tax Planning: So you can keep more of your cash in your pocket.
  • The Best News: My services are TAX DEDUCTIBLE!

Meet Kim

My name is Kim Fodrie, and I am the founder of The Intuitive Bookkeeper.

I like to think of myself as more of a profit partner working with small business owners to plan strategies for financial freedom and security.

Why Intuitive? I’m not your everyday bookkeeper. Most accounting majors are left brain processors. I am 50/50, using my creative right-brain to look into areas that are in the grey in addition to using my left-brain for detail and analytics.

I am a wife, mother, Mimi, and homeschool mom. I love spending time with my family, traveling, reading, self-development, organization, a good movie, a Netflix binge, and of course, number crunching. I absolutely love, love, love to laugh! I love working with non-profits, especially behind the scenes. Serving others and giving back is a part of who I am.

I look forward to connecting and working with you!
